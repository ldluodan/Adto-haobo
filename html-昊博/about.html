ABOUT US

Hunan Haozhi Machinery Co., Ltd. focuses on the quality manufacturing of concrete machinery and equipment. It Is located in Changsha, the capital of the world’s engineering machinery. The company integrates R&D, manufacturing, sales and service. It has a 25-year industrial elite team of concrete machinery. Service outlets all over the country, Always strive to provide customers with more advanced, more reliable, more than expected products.

The company has the capacity of research and development and manufacture of 50 spare concrete pump equipment such as stirring pump truck, pumping truck, stirring truck pump, stirring trailer pump, large vehicle pump and trailer pump, and can meet customers ‘various engineering needs.
